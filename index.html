Privacy Policy

Bay Currents for iOS

No Data Collection

Bay Currents does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, or any third-party services that collect user information.

Location

The app requests location access solely to center the map on your position. Your location is used on-device only and is never sent to any server.

Network Usage

Bay Currents retrieves ocean current forecast data from NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) via AWS CloudFront over HTTPS. These requests contain no personally identifiable information. NOAA data is in the public domain.

Offline Data

Forecast data may be cached locally on your device for offline use. This data contains only ocean current measurements and is stored in your app's private sandbox. You can clear cached data from the app's settings.

Children's Privacy

Bay Currents does not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at sfbaycurrent@gmail.com.
